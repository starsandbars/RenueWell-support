The problem

Support during recovery is
often missing.

When active treatment ends, the hustle and bustle of the hospital means that patients are often handed a survivorship plan and sent home.

But the physical after-effects of cancer treatment, like fatigue, cognitive fog, and emotional heaviness, don't end when chemotherapy does. But the structured support often does.

What support exists tends to be clinical and impersonal.

The solution

A companion for
rebuilding your life.

RenueWell was designed to be a tool bridging the time between appointments, for people navigating life after cancer treatment.

Track what matters

Log physical symptoms like fatigue, nausea, and pain alongside emotional symptoms like anxiety and loneliness, all in one app.

Rebuild healthy habits

Small, sustainable habits, like movement, nutrition, and rest, curated to help you.

Inform your care team

Generate a clear, clinical PDF report covering symptoms, trends, and emotional wellbeing, formatted to make appointment more productive.

FAQ

Is my health data backed up anywhere?
No. All data is stored exclusively on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently deleted. We recommend using iCloud device backup if you want to preserve your logs.
How do I export a PDF for my doctor?
Go to the Log tab, tap the PDF icon in the top right, choose your date range, and tap Generate. You can then share the PDF via email, AirDrop, or any other app on your phone.
Can I change the language after onboarding?
Yes. Language preferences can be updated in the app settings. RenueWell is available in English, Spanish, French, Chinese, and Portuguese.
Is RenueWell a medical app?
No. RenueWell is a personal tracking and communication tool. It does not provide diagnoses, clinical recommendations, or medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical guidance.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Email renuewellsupport@gmail.com with a description of what happened, your iPhone model, and iOS version. Screenshots help too!

Privacy policy

Your data stays
with you.

RenueWell never collects, transmits, or shares your health information. Everything you log remains on your device.

Effective date: June 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 3, 2026

1 — Who We Are

RenueWell is a personal health tracking application for iOS designed for people in cancer remission. References to "we," "us," or "our" refer to the developer of RenueWell. For questions, contact us at renuewellsupport@gmail.com.

2 — What Data the App Stores

RenueWell allows you to record and track personal health information, including:

  • Symptom logs and severity ratings
  • Mental health and emotional wellbeing scores
  • Personal habits and completion records
  • Free-text notes
  • Your name (optional, used only for PDF export headers)
  • Notification preferences
  • Your chosen display language

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device.

3 — How Your Data Is Used

The data you enter is used solely to provide the app's features: displaying your health trends, generating PDF reports for personal use or to share with your care team, and tracking your habits over time.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, machine learning, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your device.

4 — Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third party. RenueWell contains:

  • No analytics SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No crash reporting services that transmit data off-device
  • No cloud sync or remote storage
  • No user accounts or authentication services

5 — PDF Export

RenueWell can generate a PDF summary of your health data entirely on your device. We never receive, access, or store a copy of any exported PDF. Sharing via iOS's share sheet is your choice alone.

6 — Children's Privacy

RenueWell is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

7 — Data Retention and Deletion

Your data exists only on your device for as long as the app is installed. Deleting RenueWell permanently deletes all associated data. There is no server-side copy.

8 — Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security, like your passcode, Face ID, and Touch ID. Because your data never leaves your device, it is not exposed to network-based risks.

9 — Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of RenueWell after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10 — Contact

For privacy questions: renuewellsupport@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

RenueWell is not affiliated with any healthcare provider or cancer organisation. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses, clinical advice, or treatment recommendations. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical guidance.
