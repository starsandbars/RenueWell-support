Common Questions

Is my health data backed up anywhere?

No. All data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is never transmitted to any server. If you delete the app, your data is permanently deleted. We recommend using iCloud device backup if you want to preserve your logs.

How do I export a PDF to share with my doctor?

Tap the Habits tab and look for the Export option, or navigate to the PDF export screen. Enter your name (optional), choose a date range, and tap Generate. You can then share the PDF directly via the iOS share sheet — by email, AirDrop, or any other app.

Can I change the language after onboarding?

Yes. Language preferences can be updated in the app's settings. RenueWell is available in English, Spanish, French, Simplified Chinese, and Brazilian Portuguese.

What happens if I log symptoms more than once in a day?

RenueWell saves one entry per day. If you log again on the same day, your new entry replaces the previous one. Make sure your entry is complete before closing the log screen.

Is RenueWell a medical app?

No. RenueWell is a personal tracking and communication tool designed to help you record how you feel and share that information with your care team. It does not provide diagnoses, clinical recommendations, or medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical guidance.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please email us at alethea.liu09@gmail.com with a brief description of what happened and which iPhone model and iOS version you're using. Screenshots are always helpful.

Privacy Policy

Your data stays
with you.

RenueWell never collects, transmits, or shares your health information. Everything you log remains on your device.

Effective date: June 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 3, 2026

RenueWell is built on a simple principle: your health data belongs to you alone. Everything you log stays on your device. We never collect, transmit, or share your information with anyone — ever.

1 — Who We Are

RenueWell is a personal health tracking application for iOS designed for people in cancer remission. References to "we," "us," or "our" refer to the developer of RenueWell. For questions, contact us at alethea.liu09@gmail.com.

2 — What Data the App Stores

RenueWell allows you to record and track personal health information, including:

  • Symptom logs and severity ratings
  • Mental health and emotional wellbeing scores
  • Personal habits and completion records
  • Free-text notes
  • Your name (optional, used only for PDF export headers)
  • Notification preferences
  • Your chosen display language

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device.

3 — How Your Data Is Used

The data you enter is used solely to provide the app's features: displaying your health trends, generating PDF reports for personal use or to share with your care team, and tracking your habits over time.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, machine learning, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your device.

4 — Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third party. RenueWell contains:

  • No analytics SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No crash reporting services that transmit data off-device
  • No cloud sync or remote storage
  • No user accounts or authentication services

The only network activity the app performs is local notifications, handled entirely by Apple's operating system on your device.

5 — PDF Export

RenueWell can generate a PDF summary of your health data. This PDF is created entirely on your device. We never receive, access, or store a copy of any exported PDF. When you share a PDF via iOS's share sheet, it is your choice alone where that file goes.

6 — Children's Privacy

RenueWell is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

7 — Data Retention and Deletion

Your data exists only on your device for as long as the app is installed. To permanently delete all of your data, delete RenueWell from your device. iOS will remove all associated data at that point. There is no server-side copy to request deletion of.

8 — Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including passcode, Face ID, and Touch ID. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date. Because your data never leaves your device, it is not exposed to network-based risks.

9 — Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide notice within the app. Continued use of RenueWell after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
